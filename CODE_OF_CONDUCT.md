# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical substance.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or PRs that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to project maintainers.
If the issue is security-sensitive, follow `SECURITY.md`.
